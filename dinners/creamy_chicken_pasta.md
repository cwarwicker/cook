# Creamy Chicken Pasta
(Note: This recipe is for 2 servings)

## Nutritional Information
Carbs - 38g, Fats - 24g, Protein - 59g, **Calories - 604**
(approx - per serving)

## Ingredients
Tagliatelle Pasta (150g), Garlic Cloves (2), White Onion (0.5), Chicken Breast (400g), Chicken Stock (1), Creme Fraiche (200g), Butter (15g), Broccoli (1 medium), Water (100ml), Olive Oil

## Instructions

### Preparation
1. Wash and dice the broccoli into small pieces
2. Toss together with a large splash of olive oil and some salt until all covered
3. Dice 0.5 White Onion and 2 Garlic Cloves (Or use Garlic Press if you prefer)
4. Disolve chicken stock in 100ml of boiling water (use more if you want the sauce thinner)


### Cook the chicken
1. Pre-heat oven to around 200°.
2. Heat a splash of oil in a pan, medium heat.
3. Once hot, cook the chicken (400g) for 3-4 mins on 1 side, then flip and cook for another 3-4 mins, until both sides are seared. Seasoning with a touch of salt/pepper.
4. Once nicely browned on both sides, remove from pan and place in oven dish and put in oven for 20 mins.

### Cook the broccoli
1. Place prepared broccoli in oven for around 20 mins

### Cook the pasta
1. Place the pasta (150g) into a sauce pan with the boiling water and cook for 12-15 mins until soft

### Make the sauce
1. Put pre-diced onion and garlic into a pan with a splash of oil and cook for about 2 mins until starting to brown
2. Add the chicken stock (1) and butter (15g). Stir and continue to cook for another 2-3 mins
4. Add the creme fraiche (200g). Stir and continue to cook until it boils. Then reduce heat and let it simmer as it thickens a little.

### Serving
1. Once the chicken is cooked (74° internal temperature) slice into thin slices and mix into the sauce
2. Drain the pasta and either place on plate as-is with the chicken, or also mix into the sauce, depending on preference
3. Service broccoli on the side
