# Spaghetti Bolognaise
(Note: This recipe is for 2 servings)

## Nutritional Information
Carbs - 73, Fats - 37, Protein - 49, **Calories - 825**
(approx - per serving)

## Ingredients
Spaghetti (150g), Beef Mince [5%] (250g), White Onion (0.5), Garlic Cloves (2), Vegetable Stock (1), Tin of Finely Chopped Tomatoes (1), Olive Oil, Mixed Herbs (Or herbs of choice), Mature Cheddar (80g)


## Instructions

### Preparation
1. Dice 0.5 White Onion and 2 Garlic Cloves (Or use Garlic Press if you prefer)
2. Disolve vegetable stock in 100ml of boiling water

### Cook the sauce
1. Put pre-diced onion and garlic into a sauce pan with a splash of oil and cook on medium heat for about 2 mins until starting to brown
2. Add the vegetable stock and cook for another 2-3 mins
3. Add the tin of finely chopped tomatoes
4. Sprinkle herbs of choice and mix
5. Cook for around 10-15 mins. If the sauce starts to boil, bring heat down a bit

### Cook the spaghetti
1. While sauce is cooking, add spaghetti and boiling water to another sauce pan and cook for 12-15 mins until soft

### Cook the mince
1. While sauce is cooking, add mince to frying pan with a splash of olive oil and cook

### Serving
1. Once mince is cooked, pour the sauce over it and let it simmer for a few minutes on low heat
2. Place spaghetti on plate and pour mince & sauce on top
3. Top with 40g of cheddar cheese
